For Phil

Phil was strong, loyal, funny, and loved by the people around him. This is not about pretending pain is easy. It is about making sure fewer men feel they have to carry it in silence.

"Phil Stone lives on in the words of this song."

Men of Stone exists in his memory, and for every lad still here who needs a place to be real.
