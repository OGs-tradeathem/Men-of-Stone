Strength. Unity. Brotherhood.

Four nations in one mark.

The Men of Stone logo carries Wales, Scotland, Ireland, and England together. Each part matters. The point is not where a man is from; the point is that he is not left on his own.

Wales

Fire, pride, and the courage to speak before silence wins.

Scotland

Steadiness, loyalty, and turning up when it matters.

Ireland

Heart, humour, and keeping a brother close through hard days.

England

Respect, remembrance, and building something that lasts.

Run through Melksham Mental Health

Connected support, built properly.

Men of Stone will sit alongside Melksham Mental Health so the message, the meetups, and the support all connect in one place. The aim is simple: make it easier for men to reach out and easier for people to know where to send them.

More details on sessions, locations, and how to get involved will be added as the project is finalised.

How it will work

Show up. Speak if you can. Listen if you cannot.

Online

For men who need to join from home, work, or somewhere private.

In person

Face to face sessions for men nearby when the group is ready.

No judgement

A steady space where honesty matters more than having the words perfect.
